# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Activate Your Voice?
A hackathon focused on building voice-powered AI agents. Hosted at 42 Ecole Paris on February 28th - March 1st, 2026.

### Who can participate?
Anyone! Students, professionals, hobbyists - all skill levels welcome. Teams of 1-4 people.

### Is it free?
Yes, participation is completely free. Food, drinks, and snacks are provided.

---

## Tracks

### What are the four tracks?

1. **Real World Actions** - Voice agents that can perform real tasks (book appointments, control devices, trigger APIs)

2. **Persistent Memory** - Agents that remember users and context across sessions

3. **Behavioral Adaptation** - Agents that adjust their behavior based on user preferences and context

4. **Iterative Learning** - Agents that improve over time from interactions

### Do I have to pick one track?
Your project can span multiple tracks. Judges will evaluate based on whichever track(s) best fit your submission.

## Submissions

### How do I submit?
Submissions close at 5:00 PM on March 1st. Details will be shared at the partner tech talks.

### What should the submission include?
- Project name and description
- Demo video (max 3 minutes)
- GitHub repository
- Which track(s) you're entering
- Team member names

### What are the judging criteria?
- **Innovation** (25%) - Novelty of the idea
- **Technical Implementation** (25%) - Code quality and complexity
- **Use of Sponsor APIs** (25%) - Integration of Speechmatics and/or Backboard.io
- **Demo & Presentation** (25%) - Clarity and impact of demo
